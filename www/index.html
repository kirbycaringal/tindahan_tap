About the App

TindaScan is a streamlined, user-friendly Point of Sale system explicitly designed for local sari-sari stores. Built to make daily operations smoother, it transforms standard devices into powerful checkout and inventory management tools.

Key Features

  • Smart Checkout — Features built-in camera barcode scanning and manual entry for quick, hassle-free transactions.
  • Inventory Management — Easily add, update, and track product details, pricing, and barcodes directly within the app.
  • Offline Accessibility — Designed with a service worker to support offline functionality, ensuring your store keeps running even without an internet connection.
  • Clean Interface — Utilizes a highly responsive, intuitive UI tailored for quick navigation and ease of use.
